Ragnar Lothbrok is a legendary Viking hero known for his courage and determination. He is the protagonist of the series and has six children: Gyda, Bjorn Ironside, Ubbe, Hvitserk, Sigurd, and Ivar the Boneless. Ragnar, who has earned a significant place in Viking culture, stands out not only for his warrior identity but also for his exploratory and adventurous spirit. His desire to explore unknown lands by sailing Viking ships across the seas becomes a pivotal point in his career. His strategic intelligence is evident not only on the battlefield but also in the political arena, understanding power dynamics among Scandinavian tribes and becoming an effective leader within Viking communities. Ragnar undergoes a transformation in his personality over time, starting as a farmer and focusing on building his own kingdom and leading the Viking community. His dedication to northern mythology and traditions adds depth to his character. "Vikings" narrates the events that unfold in the lives of Ragnar Lothbrok and his sons, telling their compelling story.
